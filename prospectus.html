ACZ Gladiators Cricket Club Prospectus for Fundraising

Executive Summary

ACZ Gladiators Cricket Club, established in 2024, is a vibrant cricket organization based in Alpharetta, GA. We aim to foster cricket excellence and community spirit. This fundraising initiative seeks to raise $50,000 to upgrade our facilities, enhance youth programs, and support our participation in regional tournaments. Your support will help us continue our mission of promoting cricket and developing local talent.

About Us

Name of the Club: ACZ Gladiators Cricket Club

Established: 2024

Location: Alpharetta, GA

Mission Statement: To unleash cricket excellence through teamwork, respect, and relentless pursuit of improvement.

Achievements:

  • Roster of 50+ team members
  • Hosted successful youth cricket camp with 50+ participants
  • Strong participation across multiple tournaments like Paramveer T20 and T40 leages, Supreme T30 and T20 leagues, Atlanta Fire and more.
  • Organized charity match raising $5,000 for local community center

Our Objectives

  • Enhance Facilities: Upgrade our practice nets and install an electronic scoreboard.
  • Youth Development: Expand our junior cricket program to accommodate more young players.
  • Community Engagement: Organize monthly cricket workshops and family-friendly events.
  • Participation in Competitions: Enter regional tournaments to elevate our club's profile.

Why Support Us?

  • Community Impact: We provide a platform for cricket enthusiasts in Alpharetta to pursue their passion.
  • Youth Empowerment: Our junior programs focus on cricket skills and character development.
  • Promoting Diversity: Cricket brings together people from various backgrounds, fostering cultural exchange.
  • Track Record: Despite being new, we've already made significant strides in local cricket circles.

ACZ Gladiators Cricket Club

Our Mission

ACZ Gladiators is dedicated to fostering a community of passionate cricketers and sports enthusiasts. We strive to create an inclusive environment where players can grow, compete, and excel in the world of competitive cricket.

Our Vision

Our club was founded with the vision of providing a platform for cricket players of all levels to improve their skills, compete in local tournaments, and enjoy the camaraderie that comes with being part of a team.

Our History

Founded in 2023, ACZ Gladiators has quickly risen to become a prominent name in the Atlanta cricket community. Our journey began with a small group of dedicated players and has since grown into a thriving organization with multiple competitive teams across various leagues.

Our Values

  • Passion for cricket and sports
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Continuous improvement and learning
  • Respect for all members of the cricket community
  • Integrity in competition and daily operations

🏏 Match Summary: ACL CBL vs Raptors 🏏

Despite rain delays, everyone arrived on time and the umpires decided on a 16-over match.
Winning the toss, we chose to bowl first, hoping to use the wet pitch to our advantage. Unfortunately, things didn't go as planned.

Bowling Summary

  • 1st Over: 2 boundaries
  • 2nd Over: 3 boundaries
  • 3rd Over: 2 boundaries & 1 No Ball
  • 4th Over: 1 Six & 1 Boundary
  • 5th Over: 2 Boundaries
  • 6th Over: 1 boundary in Bye (otherwise only 4 singles)
  • 7th Over: 2 Sixes
  • 8th Over: 2 boundaries & 1 Six
  • 9th Over: 2 Boundaries
  • 10th Over: No Boundary (only 6 singles)
  • 11th Over: 1 Boundary, 1 No Ball, 1 Wicket
  • 12th Over: 1 No Ball, 1 Boundary, 1 Six & 2 Wickets
  • 13th Over: 2 Sixes
  • 14th Over: 2 Boundaries & 1 Six
  • 15th Over: 1 Wicket & 1 Six
  • 16th Over: 2 Sixes

Our bowling performance fell way short of expectations.
• A total of 20 boundaries and 11 sixes were conceded, allowing 146 runs solely from boundaries.
• The ball was swinging and spinning, yet we struggled to control it due to the wet conditions.

The biggest concerns:

  • Bowlers set up field placements but did not consistently bowl to the field planned with the captain.
  • Too many full tosses, some of which turned into no-balls.
  • Bowling on the batsman’s hips gave away easy runs.
  • At least 5 boundaries and 6 sixes were purely due to loose deliveries.
  • Some bowlers delivered 4 good balls but then experimented unnecessarily, conceding crucial runs.

Lack of discipline hurt us badly, with minimal dot ball pressure applied.
We need to build a mindset of finishing overs strongly without giving away freebies.
Result: Raptors scored 202 for 3 wickets.
Wickets: Trilok (2), Pavan (1), plus a runout.

Batting Performance

  • Unfortunately, our batting failed as a unit.
  • Poor shot selection and taking the game lightly cost us heavily (including my own dismissal).
  • Two unnecessary runouts were major setbacks.
  • Each batsman had the chance to play a big innings but failed to capitalize.
  • Sushruth stood out, utilizing his batting opportunity well.

Fielding Positives

  • Fielding was a bright spot with everyone putting in a solid effort.
  • Sukesh’s two catches were simply incredible.

Areas for Improvement

  • Maintain high energy levels while fielding-self-motivation is key! Mithil & Akshay tried to pump the team up from the sidelines, and Sushruth led by example on the field.
  • Play with intent and responsibility, value your own wicket when batting and bowl as per your strengths.
  • Practice hard and play confidently.

Tough day, but lessons learned 😒

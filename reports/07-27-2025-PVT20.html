🔥 Match Report: ACZ Gladiators vs Ecovue Lorven Lords

27 July 2025 | 🏆 PVCL T20 League

🟢 Toss: ACZ Gladiators won the toss and chose to bat first.

🏏 First Innings – Gladiators Go Big!

Batting first, ACZ Gladiators put on a commanding show to post 193/4 in 20 overs. Aryan Ghotkar set the tone with a strong 42 (33), partnering effectively with Alok, who anchored with 30 (35).

The fireworks came in the second half. Aiyaz Ali was in beast mode, blasting 64 off just 30 balls (8 fours, 3 sixes) at a strike rate of 213.33. Ansh Dave added the finishing touches with a blistering 26* off 10 (including 4 boundaries and a six).

  • Aiyaz Ali: 64* (30)
  • Aryan Ghotkar: 42 (33)
  • Alok Singh: 30 (35)
  • Ansh Dave: 26* (10)
  • Total: 193/4 in 20 overs
  • Extras: 24 (0b, 0lb, 24w, 0nb)

🎯 Second Innings – Anant Leads Ruthless Bowling Attack

Defending 194, the Gladiators’ bowling unit delivered a near-flawless performance. Govind Erra struck early, removing Aamer and Srinivas. The deadly spell came from Anant, who was unplayable – finishing with 6 wickets in 4 overs, derailing the middle and lower order with ruthless consistency.

Aryan got the last wicket, while Mihir, Rohith, BhavinRahul kept things tight. The Lorven Lords were bowled out for 107 in 18.4 overs, handing the Gladiators a massive 86-run victory.
